About Us

We are Nigeria's Largest Online University

Ideacloud University was established as an Open and Distance Learning (ODL) institution on February 13, 2014, offering several courses over 12 faculties.

Ideacloud University is owned and managed by Ideacloud International Limited. Its headquarters is at Victoria Island, Lagos and thwo other campuses were built in Nasarawa and Calabar states in 2016.

Ideacloud University is well recognized by the Nigeria University Council (N.U.C) and our students are enlisted in National Youth Service Corps (N.Y.S.C).
